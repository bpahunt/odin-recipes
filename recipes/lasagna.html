Classic Lasagna Recipe

Description

Prepare this easy homemade lasagne ahead of time and save in the freezer, uncooked, for when you need it during a busy week. Just bake for an extra 45 mins. Ingredients

  • 500g Quorn Mince
  • 750g Passata
  • 300g Fresh Lasagna Sheets
  • 1 Jar White Sauce
  • Lots of Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the mince
  2. Pour over the passata and season
  3. Simmer for 20 minutes
  4. In a baking dish, layer the mince, lasagna sheets and white sauce (in that order)
  5. Repeat until you run out of ingredients
  6. Cover with tasty cheese
  7. Bake for 40 minutes
  8. Eat tasty lasagna
